# Paid Intelligence

Use the Reaudit MCP tools to manage paid amplification driven by AI visibility data:

1. Use `list_visibility_gaps` to find queries where your brand is absent or underperforming
2. Use `get_visibility_gap` for detailed gap analysis with competitor data
3. Use `create_gap_campaign_brief` to generate an AI-powered campaign brief from a gap
4. Use `generate_ad_creative` to create GEO-scored ad copy for Google, Meta, LinkedIn, or X
5. Use `list_ad_creatives` and `get_ad_creative` to manage existing creatives
6. Use `create_ad_campaign` to launch a campaign with budget and schedule
7. Use `list_ad_campaigns` and `get_ad_campaign` to monitor campaign status
8. Use `list_ad_platform_connections` to check connected ad accounts
9. Use `get_visibility_lift` to see how campaigns impact AI visibility scores
10. Use `get_paid_intelligence_roi` for the full ROI dashboard

## Supported Ad Platforms

- **Google Ads** — Search RSA, Display
- **Meta Ads** — Feed, Stories, Reels, Carousel
- **LinkedIn Ads** — Sponsored Content, Message Ads, Lead Gen
- **X/Twitter Ads** — Promoted Posts, Threads
