# Content

Use the Reaudit MCP tools to help the user create and manage GEO-optimized content:

1. For content ideas, use `get_content_suggestions`
2. To generate content, use `generate_content` with type and topic
3. To view existing content, use `get_content_history` and `get_content_details`
4. To edit content, use `edit_content`
5. To translate, use `translate_content`
6. To publish, use `publish_to_wordpress` or `publish_to_react`
7. For social distribution, use `generate_social_posts` and `schedule_social_post`

## Supported content types

- `blog_post` — Long-form articles optimized for AI citation
- `faq` — Question-and-answer pages that AI engines love to cite
- `comparison` — Product/service comparisons ("X vs Y")
- `how_to` — Step-by-step guides
- `case_study` — Customer success stories
- `social_post` — Social media content
- `newsletter` — Email newsletter content
- `video_script` — Video content scripts

## Example prompts

- "Write a blog post about AI search optimization"
- "Create an FAQ about our product features"
- "Generate a comparison of our tool vs competitors"
- "Publish my latest article to WordPress"
- "Translate my blog post to Spanish"
