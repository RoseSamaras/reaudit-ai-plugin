# Competitors

Use the Reaudit MCP tools to analyze competitive AI visibility:

1. Use `list_competitors` to see tracked competitors
2. To add a competitor, use `add_competitor` with name and URL
3. To compare visibility, use `get_competitor_comparison`
4. For detailed analytics, use `query_analytics` with platform dimensions
5. To find gaps, cross-reference `get_citation_sources` with competitor mentions

## Example prompts

- "Who are my tracked competitors?"
- "Add Acme Corp as a competitor"
- "How do I compare against competitors in AI search?"
- "Which competitor has the highest AI visibility?"
- "Where do competitors outrank me in AI responses?"
